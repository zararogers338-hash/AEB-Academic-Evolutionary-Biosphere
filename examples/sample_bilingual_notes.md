# Sample Academic Notes / 样例学术笔记

Deep learning research includes representation learning, optimization, scaling laws, data quality, evaluation, and deployment.

深度学习研究包括表征学习、优化、缩放律、数据质量、评估与部署。

In an academic ecology view, some concepts become dominant species, while others remain symbiotic or disappear over time.

从学术生态视角看，一些概念会成为优势物种，另一些概念则保持共生状态或随时间消失。
